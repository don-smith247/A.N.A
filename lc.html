Important Risk Disclosure

All investments carry inherent risks and may result in loss of principal. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Please carefully consider your investment objectives, risk tolerance, and financial situation before investing. Consult with a qualified financial advisor if needed.

Terms of Service

By accessing and using A.N.A Investment's services, you agree to be bound by our terms of service. These terms outline the conditions of use, your rights and responsibilities, and our service commitments. We maintain the right to update these terms as necessary to reflect changes in our services or regulatory requirements.

Our terms cover account opening procedures, investment minimums, fee structures, withdrawal policies, and dispute resolution mechanisms. All clients are required to acknowledge and accept these terms before beginning any investment relationship with our firm.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal and financial information. Our privacy policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Your data is never sold to third parties, and we only share information when required by law or with your explicit consent.

Investment Disclosures

All investment strategies carry risks, including the potential loss of principal. We provide comprehensive risk disclosures for each investment option, including detailed information about market risks, liquidity risks, concentration risks, and other factors that may affect your investment returns.

Our investment advisory services are provided on a fiduciary basis, meaning we are legally obligated to act in your best interests. All material conflicts of interest are disclosed, and we maintain strict policies to ensure fair treatment of all clients.

Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

A.N.A Investment maintains a comprehensive Anti-Money Laundering program in compliance with federal regulations. We are committed to preventing our services from being used for money laundering or terrorist financing activities.

Our AML program includes customer identification procedures, ongoing monitoring of account activity, and reporting of suspicious transactions to appropriate authorities. All employees receive regular training on AML requirements and procedures.

Fiduciary Responsibility

As a registered investment advisor, A.N.A Investment operates under a fiduciary standard, which means we are legally and ethically obligated to act in your best interests at all times. This includes providing investment advice that is suitable for your specific financial situation and objectives.

We regularly review our fiduciary obligations and maintain detailed policies and procedures to ensure compliance. Our commitment to fiduciary responsibility extends to all aspects of our client relationships, from initial consultation through ongoing portfolio management.
